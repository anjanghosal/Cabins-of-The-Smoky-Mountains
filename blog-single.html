Gatlinburg Family Activities

Tyler Schwaeble Jun 14 • 3 min read

Ripley’s Aquarium of the Smokies: this aquarium houses over 10,000 different exotic sea creatures in 10 themed viewing galleries. Ripley’s Aquarium also features several unique experiences like Penguin Encounters where you can meet and even paint with African Penguins! More information can be found here on their website.

Ober Gatlinburg: Ober Gatlinburg is much more than your average ski run, offering a plethora of different activities which will excite each and every member of your family! The mountains are open to skiing, snowboarding and tubing; and located at the bottom of the slopes is a kid’s area where small children can play in the snow. Not to mention all of their other activities such as their Aerial Tramway, Ski Mountain Coaster, Ice Skating, Alpine Slides, Scenic Chairlift, Arcade, Carousel and even a Wildlife Encounter! Ober Gatlinburg offers more attractions during the summer and spring season, ticket prices and availability can be found here on their website.


Gatlinburg SkyLift Park: The SkyLift Park has offered Gatlinburg's best views and is now even better with the spectacular new Gatlinburg SkyBridge. The SkyBridge is the longest pedestrian suspension bridge in North America stretching 680 feet across with the midpoint suspending 140 feet over the valley of Crockett Mountain. More information can be found on their website here.

Gatlinburg Space Needle: The Gatlinburg Space Needle provides a 360 degree view of Gatlinburg and the Great Smokey Mountains from a height of 400 feet! More information can be found on their website here
