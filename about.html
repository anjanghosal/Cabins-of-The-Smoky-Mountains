Welcome

About Us

Who we are

Ghosal Properties was established back in 2011 with a main focus to build luxury properties around the country. Anjan Ghosal, our founder, is a well-traveled family man who strives for perfection in every facet of his life. He has visited over 100 different countries around the world and in doing so he discovered each country has their own definition of luxury. This sparked an idea to incorporate the different luxuries from around the globe into modern cabins. His hope was for the people staying at his lodges to experience what it’s like to live lavishly throughout the world, without traveling it

Our Purpose

We stand by our claim to have some of the most modern and technologically advances cabins in all of Tennessee. We aim to bring people and families closer by taking the stress of vacation planning out of the occasion. Our hope is to make each and every one of our visitors sense a feeling of importance, value, and luxury throughout their stay.

Where we’re going

By Summer 2021 we are planning to have over 20 different cabin offerings. These ultra-modern chic cabins will be built in clusters and will incorporate a variety of different themes. These themes will range from different decades, cultures, and even different pop-culture references. We are constantly updating our lodge offerings so make sure to explore our cabins page to see what options are currently available for your next mountain vacation.
